What Is Agentic AI?

The next evolution in artificial intelligence that goes beyond traditional GenAI capabilities.

Autonomous vs. Human-Initiated

Agentic AI systems can independently identify problems and pursue solutions without constant human prompting, unlike traditional GenAI that requires explicit instructions for each task.

Persistent Memory vs. Session-Limited

Our agents maintain context and learning across interactions, developing a growing understanding of your business needs rather than starting fresh with each conversation.

Multi-Agent Workflows vs. Stateless

Complex tasks are broken down and distributed among specialized agents that collaborate to achieve goals, contrasting with traditional GenAI's single-agent approach.

Learning from Feedback vs. Static LLMs

Our systems continuously refine their approach based on outcomes and feedback, unlike static large language models that remain fundamentally unchanged after deployment.
